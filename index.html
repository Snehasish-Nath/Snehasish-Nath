About Me

Hey there! I'm Snehasish Nath, a Web Developer driven by a passion for crafting sleek and efficient websites. My toolkit includes HTML5, CSS, Bootstrap, and JavaScript, skills I've honed through my Bachelor's in Computer Science and Engineering at IKG Punjab Technical University.
